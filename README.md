### About Me
I am a Computer Systems Engineer graduated from ITM, specializing in web and cloud technologies, as well as a Computer Support and Maintenance Technician. Currently, I work as a Software Developer at American Polo, engineering end-to-end management systems for the textile manufacturing sector.

Passionate about both software and hardware, I focus on developing custom, purpose-built systems with a perfectionist approach and a strong attention to detail. Having participated in numerous full software development lifecycles (SDLC)—from gathering requirements and design to QA, deployment, and maintenance—I have the experience to build robust solutions from the ground up. My primary focus and preference lie in Backend development, Databases, UX/UI design, and QA testing, although I am also highly capable in Frontend, DevOps, and PM.
